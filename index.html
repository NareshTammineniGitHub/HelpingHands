Our Mission

Maintain Educational Institutions like Pre-Primary, Primary School, Day Care Center, Residential Schools, Colleges, Secondary Schools, Oriented Schools, in rural villages areas.

To take over any Government/Private Schools and develop the education with all necessary infrastructure through our organization

To cater to the need of poor, suffering, widows, orphans and street children. Arranging free help to the people from voluntary organization and Government to improve the quality of living of the people.

To support meritorious and economically students, by giving educational scholarships, merit scholarship, helping in school and exam fees, to provide text books, school uniforms to poor students.

To conduct regular motivational programmes for students by inviting eminent people in the fields like, career counseling, environmental issues, importance of tree plantation, personal hygiene, and sanitation and moral values.

To conduct voluntary blood donation camps free medical camps, for students and elderly

To coordinate with various educational institutions, vocational training centers and technical training centers to help poor students.

Help to the Sick patients, Handicapped persons, Disabled persons and Mental Retardation
